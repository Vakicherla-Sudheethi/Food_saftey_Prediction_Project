Healthy Tips for You:


1. Base your meals on higher fibre starchy carbohydrates
2. Eat lots of fruit and veg
3. Eat more fish, including a portion of oily fish
4. Cut down on saturated fat and sugar Saturated fat
5. Eat less salt: no more than 6g a day for adults
6. Get active and be a healthy weight
7. Do not get thirsty
8. Do not skip breakfast




For a Healthy Diet Include:


1. Alpha Carotene rich food like Carrots, Pumpkin, Beans
2. Carbohydrates rich food like Milk, Potatoes, Corn
3. Cholesterol less food like Oats, Vegetable Oils, Egg plant and Okra
4. Fiber rich food like Wheat, Legumes, Tomatoes
5. Protein rich food like Pork, Fish, Yoghurt
6. Water rich food like Watermelon, Pineapple, Broccoli
7. Magnesium rich food like Cashews, Almonds, Tofu
8. Phosphorus rich food like Sunflower Seeds, Soy, Organ Meats
9. Potassium rich food like Spinach, Avocados, Bananas
10. Vitamin B12 rich food like Eggs, Cheese, Beef
